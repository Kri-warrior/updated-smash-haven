Srikanth Kidambi

An Indian Professional Badminton Player.

Srikanth Kidambi (born 7 February 1993) is an Indian professional badminton player.[1] A former world no. 1, Kidambi was awarded the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian award, in 2018.[2] and the Arjuna award in 2015. In 2021, he became the first Indian to reach the World Championship final in the men's singles discipline.
